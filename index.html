Philosophy

Buddhist religion is not imposed on our guests. You do not even have to be a Buddhist to come and stay as long as you wish. We teach the way of thinking, not the rules of life.

Daily Schedule

Temple is open every day from 8.00 A.M. to 8.00 P.M. Except on Friday, when it is open from 8:00 A. M. till 8:30 P.M. If it is necessary to perform.

  • 7:00 Morning Buddha Pooja followed by Chanting of Seth Pirith
  • 7:30 Breakfast for Monks
  • 8:00 Study
  • 9:00 Preferred appointment taking time
  • 11:00 Day time Buddha Pooja
  • 11:30Lunch for Monks
  • 12:30 Blessing service for devotees after lunch followed by Dhamma talk
  • 13:00 Resting
  • 14:00 Meeting by Appointment
  • 15:00 Gilanpasa Buddha Pooja and Chanting of Seth Pirith
